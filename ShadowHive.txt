PersonaTrap AI - Complete Project Scope

PROJECT NAME
ShadowHive Tagline: Autonomous Deception and Adversary Intelligence
AI-Powered Adaptive Honeypot Personalities Platform

PROBLEM STATEMENT
Traditional honeypots are often detected because they are static and unrealistic.
PersonaTrap AI generates evolving company environments, employee identities,
documents, communications, credentials, and activity patterns to create a
believable deception environment that attracts and studies attackers.

HIGH-LEVEL GOAL
Create a realistic organization that continuously evolves and appears genuine
to attackers while collecting threat intelligence and mapping attacker behavior.
